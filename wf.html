The CausalPCa Digital Twin in Action

An interactive workflow demonstrating how our causal AI augments clinical decision-making.

STEP 1: DATA SYNTHESIS

Automated Patient Record Summary

Clinician's Need

Gain a rapid, comprehensive overview of a new patient's complex history.

CausalPCa Model Action

  • Ingests and harmonizes all available data.
  • Generates a concise clinical summary.
  • Provides a draft structured radiological report.

BEHIND THE SCENES

The model fuses multimodal data (scans, labs, notes) into a unified, machine-readable format, preparing it for the causal engine.

STEP 2: PROGNOSTIC SIMULATION

"What if we wait?" — Simulating the Future

Clinician's Question

"Show the likely disease progression in 1 year without treatment."

Digital Twin Prediction

  • Generates a future MRI scan showing tumor growth.
  • Predicts key biomarker changes (e.g., PSA to 18.0).
  • Outputs a prognostic report with quantified risk.

BEHIND THE SCENES

The Neural Jump ODE (NJDE) model integrates the patient's history and evolves their latent disease state ($Z_D$) forward in continuous time to predict a future outcome.

STEP 3: CAUSAL EXPLANATION

"Why is the risk high?" — Probing the Model's Logic

Clinician's Question

"Explain the prognosis. What if this specific lesion were benign?"

Counterfactual Explanation

  • Generates a new, counterfactual scan of a benign lesion.
  • Revises prognosis to low-risk (PI-RADS 2).
  • Visually isolates the causal driver of the high-risk assessment.

BEHIND THE SCENES

The VAE performs a targeted intervention in the disentangled latent space, changing the "disease" component ($Z_D$) while keeping anatomy ($Z_A$) and patient state ($Z_P$) constant.

STEP 4: TREATMENT SIMULATION

"What if we operate?" — Comparing Treatment Paths

Clinician's Question

"Simulate a post-prostatectomy scan and predict the 6-month PSA."

Treatment Path Simulation

  • Generates a realistic post-operative PET/CT scan.
  • Predicts a 6-month PSA of <0.1 ng/mL.
  • Provides a comprehensive simulation of a positive outcome.

BEHIND THE SCENES

The NJDE applies a discrete "jump" intervention to the patient's latent state and transforms the anatomy vector ($Z_A$) to model the physical and biological effects of surgery.
